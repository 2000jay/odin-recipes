Grilled Chicken

Description

Grilled chicken is a simple and flavorful dish made by marinating chicken in a blend of oil, herbs, garlic, and spices before cooking it until tender and juicy. It has a lightly charred outer layer with a moist center and is often served with rice, salad, or roasted vegetables for a balanced meal.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breasts or thighs
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Paprika
  • Lemon juice
  • Mixed herbs

Steps

  1. Mix olive oil, garlic, lemon juice, salt, pepper, paprika, and herbs in a bowl.
  2. Coat the chicken well with the marinade.
  3. Let it rest for 20 minutes.
  4. Heat a grill pan or skillet over medium heat.
  5. Cook the chicken for 6 to 8 minutes on each side until fully cooked.
  6. Remove from heat and let it rest for a few minutes.
  7. Serve warm with vegetables or rice.
