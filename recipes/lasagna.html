Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is prepared by layering cooked pasta sheets with a rich tomato meat sauce, creamy cheese filling, and shredded mozzarella in a baking dish. The layers are repeated several times to build a thick and hearty dish, then topped with extra cheese for a golden finish. It is baked in the oven until the sauce bubbles and the cheese melts into a smooth, lightly browned crust. After resting for a few minutes, the lasagna is sliced and served warm with its rich layers clearly visible.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef or minced meat
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato sauce or crushed tomatoes
  • Tomato paste
  • Olive oil
  • Ricotta cheese or cottage cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Egg
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Italian seasoning or mixed herbs
  • Fresh parsley or basil (optional)
  • Water (if needed for sauce)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions, then drain and set aside.
  3. Heat olive oil in a pan and cook the onion and garlic until soft.
  4. Add the ground beef and cook until browned.
  5. Stir in tomato sauce, tomato paste, salt, pepper, and herbs, then simmer for 15 minutes.
  6. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, egg, and a little Parmesan cheese.
  7. Spread a layer of meat sauce in a baking dish.
  8. Add a layer of noodles, then cheese mixture, then mozzarella cheese.
  9. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used.
  10. Top with remaining sauce and extra mozzarella cheese.
  11. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  12. Remove foil and bake for another 15 minutes until golden and bubbly.
  13. Let the lasagna rest for 10 minutes before slicing and serving.
