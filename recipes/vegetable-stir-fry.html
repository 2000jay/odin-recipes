Vegetable Stir Fry

Description

Vegetable stir fry is a colorful and healthy dish made by quickly cooking fresh vegetables in a hot pan with oil, garlic, and seasonings. The vegetables remain slightly crisp while absorbing the rich flavors of the sauce, making it a light and satisfying meal often served with rice or noodles.

Ingredients

  • Broccoli
  • Carrots
  • Bell peppers
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Soy sauce
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Wash and chop all vegetables into small pieces.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan.
  3. Add onion and garlic and cook for 2 minutes.
  4. Add carrots and broccoli first, then cook for 3 minutes.
  5. Add bell peppers and stir well.
  6. Pour in soy sauce, salt, and black pepper.
  7. Cook for another 5 minutes until vegetables are tender but crisp.
  8. Serve hot with rice or noodles.
